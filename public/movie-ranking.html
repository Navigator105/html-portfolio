The Best Movies According to Terry

My top 3 movies of all-time

What about Bob?

My favourite comedy ever. So funny you just have to make sure you don't eat before watching.

The Day the Earth Stood Still (1951)

A great Sci-fi movie far ahead of its time.Excellent on every level.

Hero

An incredible kung fu epic, which is frankly amazing in every way; script, dialogue, acting, cinematography, choreography. Anabsolute masterpiece of a film.
